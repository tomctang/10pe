Calculus Review#

Ten Important Identities

  1. The quadratic equation : \(ax^2+bx+c\) has 2 roots \(r=\large\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\)

  2. Fundamental trigonometric identity : \(\cos^2{\theta}+\sin^2{\theta}=1\)

  3. Trigonometric Definitions : \(\sec{\theta}=\frac{1}{\cos{\theta}}\quad\tan{\theta}=\frac{\sin{\theta}}{\cos{\theta}}\quad\csc{\theta}=\frac{1}{\sin{\theta}}\quad\cot{\theta}=\frac{\cos{\theta}}{\sin{\theta}}\)

  4. Double Angle Formulae : \(\cos{2\theta}=\cos^2{\theta}-\sin^2{\theta}\quad\sin{2\theta}=2\sin{\theta} \cos{\theta}\)

  5. Factorization of the difference of squares : \(x^2-a^2=(x+a)(x-a)\)

  6. Geometric Series : \(\Sigma^{\infty}_{n=0}x^n=\frac{1}{1-x}\) for \(|x|<1\) and \(\Sigma^{N}_{n=0}x^n= \frac{N!}{n!(N-n)!}x^{N-n}y^n\) for all \(x\).

  7. Binomial Theorem : \((x+y)^N=\sum^{N}_{n=0}\begin{pmatrix}N\\n\end{pmatrix}x^{Nn}y^n=\Sigma^{N}_{n=0}\frac{N!}{n!(N-n)!}x^{N-n}y^n\)

  8. Power series for the exponential : \(e^x=\Sigma^{\infty}_{n=0}\frac{1}{n!}x^n\)

  9. Relation for trig functions to exponentials : \(\cos{\theta}=\frac{1}{2}(e^{i\theta}+e^{-i\theta})\) and \(\sin{\theta}=\frac{1}{2i}(e^{i\theta}-e^{-i\theta}\) n1. Relation of hyp functions to exponentials : \(\cosh{x}=\frac{1}{2}(e^x+e^{-x})\) and \(\sinh{x}=\frac{1}{2}(e^x-e^{-x})\)
