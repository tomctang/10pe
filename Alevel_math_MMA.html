7.4.4. Trigonometry Formulae#

Top right corner c means circular measure(radians).

Pythagorean Identities$\(sin^2+cos^2=1\)$$\(csc^2=cot^2+1\)$$\(sec^2=tan^2+1\)$

Addition Formulae$\(sin(A+B)=\sin{A}\cos{B}+\cos{A}\sin{B}\)$$\(cos(A+B)=\cos{A}\cos{B}-\sin{A}\sin{B}\)$$\(sin(A-B)=\sin{A}\cos{B}-\cos{A}\sin{B}\)$$\(cos(A-B)=\cos{A}\cos{B}+\sin{A}\sin{B}\)$$\(tan(A+B)=\frac{\tan{A}+\tan{B}}{1-\tan{A}\tan{B}}\$\)$\(\tan{\left( A - B \right)}=\frac{\tan{A}-\tan{B}}{1+\tan{A}\tan{B}}\)$

Double Angle Formulae$\(sin(2A)=2\sin{A}\cos{A}\)$$\(cos(2A)=\cos^2{A}-\sin^2{A}=1-2\sin^2{A}=2\cos^2{A}-1\)$$\(\tan{\left( 2A \right)}=\frac{2\tan{A}}{1-\tan^2{A}}\)$
